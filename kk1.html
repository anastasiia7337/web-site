About Ken Carson

   
   

Ken Carson, born in Atlanta, Georgia, has quickly made a name for himself in the rap industry with his unique style and captivating performances. Drawing influences from the likes of Playboi Carti and Lil Uzi Vert, Ken Carson has carved out his own niche with a fresh and energetic sound. His music reflects a blend of contemporary trap beats and innovative lyrical flows, making him a standout artist in the modern rap scene.
